Cranberry Orange Wild Rice

Description

The classic combination of cranberry and orange turn wild rice into a side dish worthy of any holiday spread. The bright, citrusy flavors of dried cranberries and oranges perfectly complement the toasty, earthy flavor of the rice, while the pecans add extra protein, flavor and a pleasant crunch. Hearty without being heavy, it’ll fill you up without weighing you down. Just one bite and you’re sure to go wild for it.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup wild rice (rinsed)
  • 1 3/4 cups water
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/8 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 cup dried cranberries
  • 1/2 cup mandarin oranges, chopped
  • 1/2 cup pecans
  • 1/4 cup orange juice

Steps

  1. In medium pot over high heat, bring water and salt to boil. Add rice and pepper; cover. Simmer 35-40 minutes, until done.
  2. Toss with dried cranberries, oranges, pecans and orange juice. Serve warm.
